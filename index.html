About



Led by London born James McGregor, who joined Seán Connelly (Guitar) and drummer Damian Greaney in early 2015, The Clockworks have had a successful few months.

Since their packed out showcase gig in Button Factory in February and the release of ‘Girls Like You’ July 2nd, they have found themselves with a slot at Whelan’s Summer Watch, one of a string of festivals and gigs this year, as well as garnering huge support from Irish music blogs and magazines: including a feature in Hot Press who claimed “you’ll be hearing a lot more about The Clockworks”. The young band have a number of headline slots around the country as well as in London over the coming months and are making sure to stay busy.

Having decided to concentrate all their efforts on music, The Clockworks have a definite mission for their future: to “write decent ‘band songs’ about everyday life” (Connelly) “and to headline Glastonbury with them” (Greaney).
